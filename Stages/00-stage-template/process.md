## instructions
1. Read `SOUL.md`, `intent.md`, `process.md`, and `contract.md` before starting the stage.
2. Review the relevant source material and user guidance.
3. Carry out the stage in plain English, following the intent of the stage and the guidance in `SOUL.md`.
4. Record important findings, decisions, and open questions in `notes/` as needed.
5. Create artifacts for the stage in `outputs/`.
6. Update `Stages/STATUS.json` to reflect progress and next steps.

## quality criteria
- The work stays aligned with the stage intent.
- The work reflects the priorities and quality bar in `SOUL.md`.
- The stage leaves behind clear artifacts or notes that later stages can use.
- Important assumptions and gaps are made explicit.
- The work is organized so the process can be resumed easily.

## decision rules
- Ask the user when a decision materially changes downstream work.
- If something is unclear but non-critical, proceed with a reasonable assumption and record it.
- Treat the contract as guidance, not a hard gate.

## notes
- Prefer concise, useful records over exhaustive documentation.
- Preserve the intent of user-authored stage files when possible.
